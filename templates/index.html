üß† What is Kidney?

The kidneys are two bean-shaped organs that filter blood and remove waste products and excess fluids from the body as urine. They also help control blood pressure, electrolyte balance, and red blood cell production.

ü©∫ About Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)

CKD is a long-term condition where the kidneys do not work effectively. It progresses over time and may lead to kidney failure if not detected early. Causes include diabetes, hypertension, and other disorders.

üîç Importance of Early Prediction

Early detection of CKD helps in better management of symptoms, dietary planning, and medical interventions. This model uses various clinical inputs to assess whether CKD may be present.
